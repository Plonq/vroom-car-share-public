Here are the most frequently asked questions about the services we provide. If your question isn't listed please contact us.

Simply access the vehicle using your VroomCard, and find the keys in the car’s information pack in the glove box. You will find the VroomCard reader on the car’s windscreen.
As a courtesy to other Vroom Car Share customers, please ensure that you return the keys and information pack to the glove box when returning your car.

You will find a fuel card in the car’s information pack in the glove box. We ask our all of our Vroom Car Share customers to be considerate of other customers, and refuel before returning the car if it is running low.
In addition, please ensure that you return the fuel card and information pack to the glove box when returning your car.

Yes please – the designated pod is the car’s home, and it must be returned there at completion of your booking to ensure continued efficient service for the next customer’s booking.

Don’t worry, simply park in the closest available legal parking spot (green sign), then call us on 03 9123 4567 and tell us the licence plate of the vehicle in the Vroom pod, and where you parked.
Do not pay any parking fees, and don’t worry about time limits, we will take care of that directly with VicRoads.

You can pay your booking any time, either in advance, during or on completion.
Please note, we require that completed bookings are paid within 14 days.

Your booking can be cancelled any time before the start time.
Please be aware that once the start time has passed, you will be charged for your booking.

Absolutely! As long as the car has not been booked by another Vroom Car Share customer, you can extend your booking either before it begins, or while it is in progress.
If the system will not allow you to extend, this likely means your car has another booking, and we ask that you ensure it is returned on time to avoid inconveniencing your fellow customer.

Yes! You will always be charged the lower rate, therefore if you extend your booking so that the hourly rate exceeds the daily rate, you will be charged the daily rate as a maximum.
For more information regarding booking costs, please visit the Pricing page.

Log in and visit the My Bookings page under the My Account menu to access your full booking history. Here you can view, extend or pay any current or upcoming bookings, cancel future bookings, and pay any outstanding bookings.
Your full booking history remains available to you, should you need to review any of your past bookings.
