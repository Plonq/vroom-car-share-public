We Believe in Sharing

With a respectful group of fellow Vroom customers, everyone gains from the benefits of sharing a vehicle rather than owning one. You simply book and use a vehicle when needed, and forego all of the costs and hassles that come with vehicle ownership. You also have versatility in vehicle choice depending on your specific need â€“ from a small zippy car for an inner city shopping trip, to a ute or a van to help you move.
Vroom Car Share also facilitates inexpensive access to a vehicle for those who may not otherwise be able to afford one.

We Care About Our Environment

Car sharing gets more privately owned cars off the road, reducing traffic congestion, energy dependency and emissions, and promoting cleaner air. It also reduces wear and tear on our roads and lessens the need for parking infrastructure, with the long term potential benefits being the ability to divert resources to the creation of more parklands and urban green spaces.
The indirect benefit to our customers is improved health due to increased walking or cycling to get around, rather than habitually jumping in the car. Even if it is just that short walk to the nearest Vroom Car Share pod!
