Vroom has a range of cars to suit every purpose and budget

Each of our vehicles is assigned an hourly and a daily rate, and you will always pay the lesser calculated cost for your booking. This means that if you book an Economy car for 6 hours or more per day of your booking, you will only pay $50 per day.

Rates are inclusive of all on-road costs such as fuel, registration, insurance, cleaning and servicing. Both daily and hourly rates are inclusive of unlimited kilometres.
